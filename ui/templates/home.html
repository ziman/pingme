Pingme

Pingme is a service that reminds you of things. Just send or forward an email to pingme-2013-01-01@functor.sk or pingme-2013-01-01-1400@functor.sk and it will return to you at the time you specified in the address.

Note that the punctuation after the pingme- prefix is left completely up to you — it is erased during processing so you can use any time/date delimiters you want. The only thing you need to keep is the ordering of the numbers: YYYY, MM, DD[, hh, mm].
